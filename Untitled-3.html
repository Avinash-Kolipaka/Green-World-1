What is Environmental sustainability?

Environmental sustainability is like being the stewards of Earth, ensuring we safeguard its resources and ecosystems for the well-being of current and future generations. It's not just about meeting our needs today; it's about doing so in a way that leaves the planet thriving for those who come after us. It's a responsibility with a ripple effect that extends beyond our immediate actions.
